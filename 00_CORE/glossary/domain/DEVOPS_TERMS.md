# DevOps Domain Glossary

---
term: error_budget
category: devops
definition: The maximum amount of time a technical service can fail without violating the Service Level Agreement (SLA).
aliases: []
examples:
  - 99.9% uptime translates to 43.8 minutes error budget per month.
notes: Managed by SRE/DevOps agents to balance speed vs stability.
related_terms:
  - SLO
  - SLA
---
